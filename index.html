About the Game

Puppy Diary: Epic Match 3 is a fun and heartwarming match-3 puzzle game where players join a cute puppy on a colorful journey through various levels filled with exciting challenges. Match blocks, clear obstacles, and help your puppy collect treats and toys. With each level, unlock new abilities and encounter charming characters as you progress through the game.

Game Features

  • Explore a world full of vibrant colors, adorable puppies, and fun challenges.
  • Play hundreds of levels with diverse puzzles and evolving objectives.
  • Unlock special puppy powers and boosters to help you on your journey.
  • Enjoy a cute and engaging storyline filled with charming characters and puppy adventures.
